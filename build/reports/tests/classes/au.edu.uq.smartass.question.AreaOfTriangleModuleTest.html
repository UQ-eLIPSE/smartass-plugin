$\frac{\sqrt{81.0}}{2}$ units$^2$
\begin{align*}
\text{Area}&=\frac{1}{2}|\vec{AB}\times\vec{AC}|\\
\vec{AB}&=(3,4,5)\\
\vec{AC}&=(1,0,2)\\\\
\vec{AB}\times\vec{AC}&=\left|\begin{array}{crc}\textbf{i}&\textbf{j}&\textbf{k}\\3&4&5\\1&0&2\end{array}\right|\\
&=\textbf{i}\left|\begin{array}{rc}4&5\\0&2\end{array}\right|-\textbf{j}\left|\begin{array}{cc}3&5\\1&2\end{array}\right|+\textbf{k}\left|\begin{array}{cr}3&4\\1&0\end{array} \right|\\

|\vec{AB}\times\vec{AC}|&=\sqrt{8^2+1^2+4^2}\\
&=\sqrt{81.0}\\\\
